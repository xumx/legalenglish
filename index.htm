About Legal-Ease Seminars

Training Solutions

Legal-Ease International is a company that specializes in training Legal English to attorneys throughout the world. Our instructors are American licensed attorneys with vast experience teaching Live seminars in Legal English. Legal-Ease International has taught thousands of lawyers and businesses throughout Europe, Asia, Mexico and South America. That is why we are the World Leader in Legal English®. Live classes, by real lawyers!

Main goals of the seminar

The course will cover the appropriate terminology and structure to be able to create contracts in Legal English. The exercises provided in class will be personally corrected by our experienced instructors.

The course also covers a synopsis of the legislative and judicial system of the United States

Learning Objectives

  • Commercial Terminology
  • Letters and Contracts
  • International Transactions
  • Dispute Resolution
  • Corporate Law
